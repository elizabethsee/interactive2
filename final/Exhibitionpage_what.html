ELLSWORTH KELLY

An exhibition in form

“An exhibition in form” features three of Kelly’s pieces in an interactive sense whereby the focus is the texture and the materiality of Kelly’s sculptures. It looks at the minute details of his materiality through the abstraction of forms and shapes, referencing the forms in Kelly's body of work.

Held at the SFMOMA, exhibition opens May 21, 2019 until September 2, 2019.
